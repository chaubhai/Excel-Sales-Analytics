# Excel-Sales-Analytics
## Sales Report:
**Project Objective:**  
**1.** Create a _[Customer Peroformance Report](https://github.com/chaubhai/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)_  
**2.** Conduct a comprehensive comparison between _[Market Performance and Sales Targets](https://github.com/chaubhai/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Performance%20vs%20Targert%20Report.pdf)_

**Purpose of sales analytics:** Enable companies to track and assess their performance and sales efforts.  
**Importance of analyzing sales data:** Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and spot sales trends.  
**Role of reports:** Identify possible corporate expansion prospects in promising nations, negotiate with customers, and determine effective customer discounts.

## Finance Report:
**Project Objective:**  
**1.** Create Profit and Loss (P & L) reports by _[Fiscal Year](https://github.com/chaubhai/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf)_ & _[Months](https://github.com/chaubhai/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Years%2C%20Quarters%2C%20and%20Months.pdf)_.  
**2.** Create Profit and Loss (P & L) report by _[Markets](https://github.com/chaubhai/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20Statement%20my%20Market.pdf)_.  

**Purpose of financial analytics:** Financial performance evaluation, decision-making assistance, and stakeholder communication facilitation.  
**Importance of analyzing finance data:** Support benchmarking against peers in the industry and historical periods as a basis for forecasting and budgeting.  
**Role of reports:** Align strategic objectives with financial planning and encourage trust in the company's financial future.
